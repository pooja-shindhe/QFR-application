How It Works

For Customers

  1. Register as a Customer
  2. Create detailed RFQ with specifications
  3. Receive bids from multiple vendors
  4. Compare quotes and select best vendor
  5. Award contract and manage delivery

For Vendors

  1. Register as a Vendor
  2. Browse open RFQs in your domain
  3. Submit competitive bids with pricing
  4. Track bid status and customer feedback
  5. Win contracts and fulfill orders
